frisson

you creep up on me from nowhere
started as a mere sensation
just slight and
in the background

but now
you radiate
cylindrically, dimensionally
through me

pure emotion
not tainted with words
or others
just me and you

entwined, while you
wash me
cleaning me from the shell
of ordinary life

it's not quite normal
I don't really know if it's
me feeling you, or
you feeling me

but I like it
you can abstract me away from reality
in a unique way that paradoxically
brings me straight back

and then when I think of you
you radiate further,
feeding yourself
and refreshing me

you once were small
but you grew from me
pulsating
and resonating sweetly
back
