existential reflections on existence

and as the light gleams through the window
you lose force colliding symmetrically with the glass
you spew prism, mass collection
tearing beauty upon the world


who knew that you could compose so much
your poker hair, your twisted face
your expression vocalises harmoniously
what your face once forgot


a distorted set of component beams
constructs the view once known as me
i lose depth, i lose faith
but a self is strengthened


the keys go klicker-klack
bitterly reflecting away
i look into your sunken tomes
while the days sway


i look into the glassen eyes
we pretend that consciousness is passed
through mutual ignorance of bitter lakes
distorted truths flow through


sunken surfaces, rolling views
your tepid glass lets through
lacking the solidity of a swampy lake
but holding strong resistance to a shoe


we look and see and look and be
i draw resolute you draw frustration
we are what we are and we be what we be
though we both are as hollow as each other.
