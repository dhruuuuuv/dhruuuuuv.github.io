it was supposed to be different


the idea
of a situation spills into
every single aspect
of your being

it takes you over
it alluringly pulls you away from the actuality
sucking on you
feeding on you

and you can give it nothing
but attention
you are drawn in by its shine
the magnanimity of its colours

until it is no longer an unwelcome guest
we happily let it in
and feed it until it is full
all so we won't be alone with the bitterness of reality

the idea
sits patiently
slowing but assuredly worming
to the very roots of the brain

time ticks
and tocks
and passes on and on
the moment finally arrives

...
"oh" you say
I didn't want this
it was supposed to be different

you show to yourself the brochure
of the idea
and say
it was supposed to be different

you sit discontented
with the actuality
of reality
it was supposed to be different

and then,
quietly and politely
the next idea drifts in
and makes itself a welcome guest

it pulls up a chair
schemes its plan
waits on and on for you to say
it was supposed to be different
back
