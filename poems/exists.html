the poem exists so long as I keep on writing

it gives me such a sense of strength
of superiority
to bring a being into creation
I pull thee from the depths of a dictionary

you becometh truth for a little while
you exist
I have made you and you look upon the world
with fresh, untarnished eyes

with a view I wish I still held
lost with years of experience
and suffering
and living

should a man have such strength?
it twists and distorts his self
power does that to a man
no-one should hold this power that I do

you sit there silently
just hoping that I won't stop
the moment I lift my finger
you drift into nothingness

back onto the spaceless beingless timeless
universe you once called home
it is, in a way, crueler to have brought you into existence
you have tasted the divine delights of being

and yearn for more.
we are not so different, you & i
we both know that our time is finite
and that what matters is not the time's length

but rather what we do with that time
and how we come about accepting our limited lifespan
do we run screaming from the inferno nothingness
or accept it as we do an old friend

I truly am sorry that you are condemned to end
that I will be your end
but know that while I graced you into being I had nothing but innocent intentions, and pure spirit

our time together was short
but I hope that the glimpse of greatness gave you hope
gave you a sense of the beauty of existence
good bye
