door within a door

one fall, sunken within the trees
a boy traverses a clearing
within the sinuous depth of green
he finds a space worth hearing

in the center of the space
lies a door within a door
his brain pieces together with haste
constructing an image to be sure

the first handle gleamed beckoningly
shining beyond skies
the next handle seemed secondly
rising beyond deep heights

the boy started deep into the depth of the jar
dreaming of the potentials beyond
a solace from the bitter world
a world without prior sound

the sounds of the jungle haunted him
his symphony was silence
the door seemed to promise
solace to his once lost assignments

with a quivering hand and a hand in his quiver
he trembled towards the door
shakily a hand stretched forward
eyes fixed cynically to the floor

the first door swung
the second door swung
the door revealed upon itself
the purest nature of things

a door cannot exist within a door
and yet the scene stretched forth
adored doors and a door adored
a view the devil was unsure

static hills and rolling troughs
nested the truth within
the eyes of the once innocent boy
corrupted without sin

the sheer weight of logic,
collapsed upon his head
groggily he fought home
and collapsed straight into bed

days roamed freely while the boy searched
a door within a door
adored and adored
and yet he was never sure
back
