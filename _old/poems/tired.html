tired

i look upon the world
and it's slightly faded
slightly askew
like viewed through frosted glass.

it's a false perception
whatever a false perception means,
but it's not real:
i therefore nothing is.

i sip on coffee and water.
the world regains slight clarity.
but you're still slightly shifted out of sync
with me.

because according to me and the way i write
i am all that exists.
no-one else.
i am the beginning and the end.

and also the middle,
those tiny bits that others look upon
with a scorn on their face.
but i think it's all real.

the world is not here.
in honesty it doesn't matter if i am
final, or not.
because it ticks and tocks

and i pass by.
just watching
just waiting
for the world to kick in.

so i yearn for the moments when reality doesn't seem faded.
when the sun pours on me and
i am happy.
and i am no longer tired.
