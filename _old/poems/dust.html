sparkling dust
you roam freely, sweetly
lost into the world
until you're beaten by sunlight

the beams give you life
smash you into existence
you bleed hope
you exist

whilst lost, terribly unsure
if the world continues or ceases while
you are hidden
does it matter ?

the bolder of you believe that
all revolves around you
while you are not there
you are all that revolves

and yet, that chaotic moment
where the cruel iridescence
observes you into existence
is beautiful

you are brought fresh
clean, pure, into a world that you
know nothing of
and therefore collect hope

you drift
assuredly
lazily
enjoying the excitement of pure existence

knowing so little of the sweet pain
of existence
but that does not matter
you capture colour and swirl it

it explodes all around you
you keep the ones you need
and once full
hurl back the rest

beings undermine you
as being small and insignificance
but mere existence
gives you more significance

more than your former siblings
could ever lazily dream of
